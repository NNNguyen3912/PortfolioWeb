About Project

I initially planned to develop this project as an MMORPG. However, realizing the heavy workload and my limited resources as an individual, I decided to focus on optimizing the most important core: the Combat System. Instead of building a vast but shallow world, I chose to extract the Hack & Slash aspect to focus on handling control mechanics, hit feeling (impact), and visual effects, aiming to create a gameplay experience that is as smooth and complete as possible within my current capabilities.

World Design Description

Direction: Developing the world using a Linear Maps structure.

Design Mindset: Instead of building an Open World that exceeds the optimization capabilities of a single person, I focus on creating zones with clear paths. This approach helps me better control the player experience, ensuring that each scene is carefully crafted in terms of environment and lighting.

Map Structure: The areas are divided into smaller interconnected segments (similar to the structure of Toram Online). This not only reduces system resource usage but also allows me to design smooth transition points, from forest trails to dark and majestic caves.

Environmental Contrast: I emphasize changing the atmosphere between areas to give players a sense of progression. By shifting from the warm yellow light of mountain forests to the cold, misty blue tones of sealed lands, I aim to spark curiosity and encourage players to explore what lies ahead.

Combat System Description


Attacks & Visual Effects (VFX)

Instead of spreading features too thin, I focused on perfecting the character's core skill set to provide the best possible visual experience:

Melee Combat Focus: The main gameplay revolves around high-speed melee attack sequences.

Impactful AOE: Integrated Area of Effect (AOE) skills with distinct visuals (magic circles, feather effects) for finishing moves or escaping when surrounded, creating visual highlights during combat.

Visual Feedback: I use particle systems and screen shake to simulate the power of skills, helping players clearly feel the impact whenever a major attack is unleashed.

Advanced Boss Mechanics

This is the part I put the most effort into, aiming to create a real challenge for players:

Complex Skill Sets: Bosses are programmed with various attack types—from quick dashes and AOE attacks to long-range projectiles—forcing players to constantly switch between offensive and defensive tactics.

Phase Transition: Bosses have the ability to transition between different states (Phases). This not only increases the difficulty but also changes the combat rhythm, requiring players to quickly adapt to new attack patterns.

Pattern Recognition: The design emphasizes observation; every boss skill has specific telegraphing signs so players can actively dash/dodge and find openings to counter-attack.
